Our Estate

A family, a hillside,
a hundred harvests.

In 1962, Elena and Thomas Nightingale planted their first rows of cabernet on a windswept slope above the valley. They believed great wine is not made in a cellar but grown in the ground, season by season, with hands that know each vine.

Today we farm sustainably, harvest by hand, and bottle only what the land gives us. No shortcuts, no compromises, just honest wine that tastes of this singular place.
